Onion Rings

Description

Onion rings are a delicous snack or side to your meal, they provide a crispy texture with hint of a salty taste, perfect to satisfy your cravings. This recipe takes around 15 minutes to prepare, and 20 minutes to cook. This fast recipe will provide you with a snack everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole onion
  • 1 cup, all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp of cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Slice the onion into 1/4 inch thick rings
  2. Delicatly seperate the rings onto a plate
  3. In a bowl add 1 cup of all purpose flour, 1/2 tsp of cayenne pepper, 1/4 tsp of salt, and 1/4 tsp of pepper
  4. Slowly add the cup of water to the bowl with ingredients, and whisk until it becomes a nice smooth batter
  5. Place the sliced onions into the batter, then place into the bowl with the breadcrumbs, toss the onions until they are fully coated
  6. Place the onions covered in batter and bread crumbs into hot oil for approximatly 1 minute, or until golden brown
  7. Enjoy your delicious onion rings!
