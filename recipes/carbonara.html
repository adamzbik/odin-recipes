Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a hot pasta tossed with a creamy sauce of raw beaten eggs, mixed with cured pork and cheese, this savoury dish is perfect for all pasta lovers.

Ingredients

  • 4 oz of bacon or pancetta
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 box of spaghetti
  • 2 oz parmesan

Steps

  1. Heat water in a large pot until it boils, place the pasta in until it is soft
  2. While the pasta is cooking, cut up the bacon into small strips
  3. Whisk 4 eggs into a bowl, and place cheese and pepper in, mix until no streaks remain
  4. In a seperate pan place the cut bacon, and cook until golden brown
  5. Once the pasta is cooked place it into the pan with the bacon, aswell as pour the eggs in. Mix for around 1 minute
  6. Enjoy!
